6 of 6 · Gayming Icon 2024
Jeremy Crawford

Eighteen years at Wizards of the Coast (2007-2025). Lead Rules Designer of D&D 4th edition. Co-Lead Designer of 5e with Mike Mearls and the architect of the modern D&D era. Lead designer of the 2024 Player's Handbook revision. Game Director when he left.

Co-created Blue Rose (2005) with Steve Kenson before WotC. That book won the 2005 ENnies for Best Rules and Best d20 Game.

Openly gay. Married. Received the Gayming Icon Award in 2024 for his impact on game design and LGBTQ+ visibility. The crew of six on June 8 is going to the Gayming Awards with an actual Gayming Icon among us.

Now Game Director at Darrington Press, Critical Role's tabletop imprint. Working on Daggerheart and new game concepts. Joined June 2025 alongside Chris Perkins. His own line: "Great games invite everyone to the table."

Lunch June 4 at Novo Cafe. Awards Monday June 8.
