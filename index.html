The shape of the month

Four LA waves, one Chicago weekend, one Wisconsin landing.

June is dense this year. Five distinct gatherings of the people I love, woven through one month, ending in Chicago Pride with Eric Francksen, his wife Crystal, and the family. Eric's first ever Pride. That is the thing I am holding closest of all.

The shape of the month, in five lines:

  • Wave 1 · Jun 5-8. Pink Pony Rodeo at the LA LGBT Center with Joe. Summer Game Fest. Gayming Awards with the crew of six.
  • Wave 2 · Jun 11-15. Quinn drives up from San Diego. The opener. Mason's birthday. LA Pride Parade Sunday.
  • Wave 3 · Jun 19-22. Drake and Mel down from Winnipeg with the rave fam. Chris Lake Saturday. Drake's birthday Sunday Funday at our house.
  • Wave 4 · Jun 23-26. Quinn drives up again. USA vs Türkiye with Pete Hines. Three nights of soccer chosen-family.
  • Wave 5 · Jun 26 → Jul 5. Fly to Chicago. Pokemon Museum and the Minecraft Experience Saturday with the Francksen boys. Chicago Pride Sunday Jun 28. Then the Dragon House.
2026 Pride · the theme

For All of Us.

This is the official Pride 2026 theme. It is the one we should be saying out loud. "No one is free until everyone is free."

The political climate in 2026 is rougher. Federal rollback, sponsor pullback, the trans community still bearing the heaviest cost. Pride this year is more political than corporate. The activists are back at the front of the march. The corporate logos are quieter, the community is louder.

What that means for me is simple. The people I am gathering this month are the answer. Joe. Mason and Mychal. Eric and Crystal. Drake and Mel. Quinn. Brennan in Brooklyn. Foster about to host me in New York for the Final. The Gayming Awards crew of six who all sit at the same Pride table.

This site is partly for me. It is mostly for you.
